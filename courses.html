TrackPass lists 100+ public and municipal golf courses across Texas -- Austin, San Antonio, Dallas, Fort Worth, Houston, El Paso, Lubbock, Amarillo, Corpus Christi, and 25+ other cities. TrackPass members ($199/year) get 1 free round per year per course at partner (in-network) courses. At any other Texas public course (out-of-network), members pay the green fee, log the round, and submit the receipt — TrackPass reimburses up to $50 per round, 1 round per month, up to $199 per membership year.
