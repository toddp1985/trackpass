Fort Worth golf — common questions

How many public golf courses are in Fort Worth, Texas?

4 Fort Worth-area public courses are in the TrackPass directory: Pecan Valley Golf Course — River, Rockwood Park Golf Course, Meadowbrook Golf Course, Pecan Valley Golf Course — Hills. Green fees run about $28–$40 a round.

What is the cheapest way to play golf in Fort Worth?

Municipal and daily-fee courses are the cheapest tee times in Fort Worth. Green fees run about $28–$40 a round. A TrackPass membership ($199/year) reimburses your green fee up to $50 a round — one round a month, up to $199 back per year — which can cover most of what a regular Fort Worth golfer spends on green fees.

How does TrackPass work at Fort Worth courses?

Pay the green fee at any Fort Worth public course like normal, log the round in your TrackPass dashboard, and email the receipt to reimbursements@trackpassgolf.com. You're reimbursed up to $50 for that round (one round per month, capped at $199 per membership year). At partner courses you get 1 free round a year — just show your pass.

Do I need to book tee times through TrackPass?

No. Book directly with the course the way you always do — by phone or their online tee sheet. TrackPass isn't a booking site; it pays you back after you play.
