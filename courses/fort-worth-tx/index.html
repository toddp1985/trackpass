About TrackPass

TrackPass ($199/year) is a flat-rate golf pass covering every public golf course in Texas. Partner courses: 2 free rounds/yr per course. Any other Texas public course: pay the green fee, log the round, and submit the receipt for reimbursement — up to $50/round, 1 round/month, up to $199/year. One QR code, 95 courses.
