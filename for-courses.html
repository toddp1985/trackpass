Common questions

Before you fill out the form, here's what pro shop managers usually ask.

What does this cost the course?

Nothing. Zero. There is no fee to join TrackPass and no monthly subscription. We only make money when members pay the annual pass fee — and we use that to pay you when they play your course.

Do we have to change our rates?

No. TrackPass members pay your standard published green fee rate. We pay it on their behalf. Your pricing stays exactly the same.

What does the staff have to do?

The member shows a QR code on their phone. Your staff opens the check-in page on any device, scans or enters the member ID, and confirms it's valid. That's it — takes about 10 seconds. No special hardware, no app to install.

What if a member shows up and we're fully booked?

TrackPass doesn't guarantee tee times or pre-book slots. Members show up and book the same way any walk-in does. If you're full, you're full — just like any other day. You're in control of your course.

Can we leave the network if it's not working?

Yes. This isn't a contract. If you want to stop being listed, contact us and you'll be removed. No penalty, no fees.
