Frequently Asked Questions

What is TrackPass? expand_more
TrackPass is a $199/year Texas golf pass. At partner (in-network) courses: 2 free rounds per year per course — just show your digital pass at the pro shop, no receipt needed. At any other Texas public course: 1 free round per year per course — show your pass and play, no receipt needed.
How does the reimbursement work at courses not in the network? expand_more
Show your TrackPass pass at any out-of-network Texas public course and play free — 1 free round per year per course. No receipt needed, no reimbursement process. At partner courses you get 2 free rounds per year per course, same process.
Are partner courses really free? expand_more
Yes. At any partner course, show your digital pass QR code at the pro shop. No green fee, no receipt needed. Each partner course is free up to 2 rounds per year.
Can I cancel? expand_more
Yes — cancel before launch for a full refund. After launch, TrackPass is an annual pass. Founding members lock in $199 for their first season.
When does TrackPass launch? expand_more
We're in the founding-member phase now. Founding members who buy at $199 lock in their rate and get access on launch day. We'll email you with the launch date.
