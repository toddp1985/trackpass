Frequently Asked Questions

What is TrackPass? expand_more
TrackPass is a $199/year Texas golf pass. At partner (in-network) courses: 1 free round per year per course — just show your digital pass at the pro shop. At any other Texas public course: pay your green fee, log your round, and send us the receipt — we reimburse up to $50 per round, 1 round per month, up to $199 per membership year.
How does it work at courses not in the partner network? expand_more
Play any out-of-network Texas public course, pay the green fee, then log your round from your member dashboard and email the receipt to reimbursements@trackpassgolf.com. We reimburse up to $50 per round — 1 round per month, up to $199 per membership year. At partner courses, just show your pass and play free — 1 round per year per course.
Are partner courses really free? expand_more
Yes. At any partner course, show your digital pass QR code at the pro shop. No green fee. Each partner course is free up to 1 round per year.
Can I cancel? expand_more
Yes — cancel within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. After 30 days, TrackPass is an annual pass.
Is TrackPass live? expand_more
Yes — TrackPass is live and accepting members. Buy at $199/year (Solo) or $349/year (Family) via Stripe. Your digital pass is delivered instantly after checkout.
