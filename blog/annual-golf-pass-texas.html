TrackPass is the first annual golf pass that works at every public course in Texas. Here's how it works and whether it's worth it for you.

Published June 2026 · TrackPass

Texas golfers who play public courses have historically had two choices: pay full green fee every round, or buy a city season pass that only covers courses in one metro area. TrackPass is a third option -- a $199/year annual golf pass that works at any public course in Texas.

How TrackPass Works

One pass. Two types of coverage:

star Partner Courses

Show your TrackPass at the pro shop. Play free. No receipt, no form, no waiting. Up to 2 rounds per year at each partner course.

golf_course Any Other TX Course

Pay the green fee, log your round in the member dashboard, and submit your receipt. TrackPass reimburses up to $50/round, 1 round per month, up to $199 per membership year. Covers every Texas public course not in the partner network.
