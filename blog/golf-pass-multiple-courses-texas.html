Golf Pass for Multiple Courses in Texas: How TrackPass Works in 2026

City season passes only cover one city's courses. TrackPass covers every public course in Texas for $199/year. Here's how it works.

Published June 2026 · TrackPass

If you golf in more than one Texas city, or you want to try different public courses without paying full green fee every time, city season passes don't solve your problem. They lock you into one set of courses. TrackPass is the only pass that covers multiple Texas golf courses across the entire state.

Why a Multi-Course Golf Pass Matters

Texas golfers travel. A golfer based in Austin regularly plays courses in San Antonio on weekends, makes trips to Dallas, and heads to Houston for work. A city-specific season pass -- even at $600-900/year -- only covers 5-8 courses in one metro area. Every round outside that metro costs full green fee on top of the season pass cost.

TrackPass works at any Texas public course by design. It was built specifically to cover the golfer who wants to play around the state without a separate payment arrangement at each new course.

How TrackPass Covers Multiple Courses

Two coverage tiers, one pass:

star Partner (in-network) courses

1 free round per year per course. Show your TrackPass digital pass at the pro shop, no receipt, no form. The course logs your visit and you play. Partner courses include munis and public courses across Austin, Dallas, Houston, San Antonio, and other Texas cities.

golf_course Any other Texas public course

Pay the green fee, log your round in the member dashboard, and email the receipt to reimbursements@trackpassgolf.com. TrackPass reimburses up to $50/round, 1 round per month, up to $199 per membership year. This covers every Texas public course not in the partner network, which includes 570+ courses statewide.
