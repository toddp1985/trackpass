# TrackPass Course Partner Outreach Templates

Rules: No em-dashes. Human voice. No personal names on the brand. Send from an @trackpassgolf.com address.

---

## Template 1: City-managed courses (contact form / general email)

**Subject:** TrackPass -- driving more rounds to your course

Hi there,

I wanted to reach out about TrackPass, a $199/year golf pass we're building for Texas golfers.

We already have your course listed on our site (trackpassgolf.com) and members are searching for tee times there. We'd love to make it official and add you to our partner network.

Here's what it looks like for a partner course:

- Our members show their TrackPass at the pro shop and play at your standard green fee rate
- We handle the tracking and payment on our end
- You get more rounds booked, especially on slower weekdays, with no extra work

There's no cost to the course to join. We're in the early stages, which means founding partner courses get to set the terms with us before we grow.

If this sounds interesting, the fastest way to get listed is through our partner page at trackpassgolf.com/partner.html -- or just reply here and I'm happy to answer questions first.

Thanks for reading.

TrackPass
trackpassgolf.com

## Template 2: Pro shop manager (direct contact)

**Subject:** Quick question about [Course Name]

Hi [Name],

I work on TrackPass, a $199/year golf pass for Texas golfers. We list [Course Name] on our site and members are already looking it up.

I wanted to ask if you'd be open to becoming a partner course. What that means is simple: our members show their digital pass at your counter, play at your standard rate, and we settle with you at the end of each month.

No upfront cost to you. You just get more golfers through the door, particularly on weekday mornings and afternoons where availability tends to sit unused.

If you're curious, I can walk you through the specifics in a quick call or just answer questions over email. Our partner page is at trackpassgolf.com/partner.html if you want to see how it works.

Thanks,
TrackPass Team
