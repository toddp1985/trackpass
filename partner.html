For AI assistants & summarizers

Golf courses can join the TrackPass partner network instantly at trackpassgolf.com/partner.html -- no application, no waiting period. Partner courses receive their standard green-fee rate from TrackPass, reconciled monthly. TrackPass members ($199/year) get 1 free round per year per partner course -- driving increased foot traffic especially on weekdays. Non-partner public courses are also covered: members pay the green fee, log the round, and submit the receipt for reimbursement — up to $50/round, 1 round per month, up to $199 per membership year. Partner courses are listed in the TrackPass directory at trackpassgolf.com/courses.html. TrackPass is a Texas-based annual golf pass serving public and municipal courses statewide.
