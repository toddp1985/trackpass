The deal

Built for courses with open inventory

1
The problem

Corporate booking apps take a cut of every green fee just to list you — and conglomerates price out everyday golfers.

2
The solution

A multi-course pass that sends passholders to your weekday and off-peak tee sheet — capped at 2 visits each, so you never lose your regulars.

3
How you get paid

Every passholder round is logged by QR check-in. At season's end you get a guaranteed lump-sum check from the escrow pool — plus 100% of everything they spend on-site.

Why it works

You only fill what's empty

  • Redemptions are weekday & off-peak, advance-booked — they fill tee times that would otherwise go empty, not displace paying weekend play.
  • Capped at 2 rounds per passholder, per season — no one cannibalizes your loyal locals.
  • You set blackout dates. You keep your rack rate. You never discount walk-ups.
  • Geographic "breakage" means most passholders never visit every course — which makes the per-round check generous.
