Frequently Asked Questions

What is TrackPass? expand_more
TrackPass is a $199/year Texas golf pass. At partner (in-network) courses: 2 free rounds per year per course — just show your digital pass at the pro shop. At any other Texas public course: pay your green fee, log your round, and send us the receipt — we reimburse up to $50 per round, 1 round per month, up to $199 per membership year.
How does it work at courses not in the partner network? expand_more
Play any out-of-network Texas public course, pay the green fee, then log your round from your member dashboard and email the receipt to reimbursements@trackpassgolf.com. We reimburse up to $50 per round — 1 round per month, up to $199 per membership year. At partner courses it's even simpler: show your pass at the pro shop and play free, 2 rounds per year per course.
Are partner courses really free? expand_more
Yes. At any course in the TrackPass partner network, you show your digital pass QR code at the pro shop — no green fee charged. Each partner course is free up to 2 rounds per year. You still pay for cart rental and anything else you want. (We're actively signing partner courses across Texas — out-of-network reimbursement covers you everywhere else in the meantime.)
Can I cancel? expand_more
Yes — you can cancel within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. After 30 days, TrackPass is an annual pass (no mid-year refunds after the membership activates, per standard terms).
Is TrackPass live? expand_more
Yes — TrackPass is live and accepting members. Buy at $199/year (Solo) or $349/year (Family) via Stripe. Your digital pass is delivered instantly after checkout. We're actively adding partner courses across Austin, San Antonio, Dallas–Fort Worth, and Houston.
